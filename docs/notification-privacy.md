# Notification Privacy Guide

## Why Notification Privacy Matters

Notifications are useful, but they can also reveal personal information to anyone who glances at your phone.

Whether you're at work, with friends, or sharing your device with family members, message previews may expose conversations you intended to keep private.

## Tips to Protect Notification Privacy

- Disable message previews on the lock screen.
- Use app-specific notification settings.
- Lock your device when not in use.
- Avoid displaying sensitive conversations on shared screens.
- Consider privacy-focused messaging apps for private communication.
